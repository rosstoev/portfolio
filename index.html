About me

Hello, my name is Rosen Stoev. I graduated and started working and although I managed to make good money, I did not feel satisfied enough. I needed something different to build a career in, something that I would enjoy and be interested in. I am spending most of my free time in front of the computer and actually I have always been keen on computers. So, one day I just thought that maybe this is exactly what I am really good at and should work on to develop. At first, I started drawing in Photoshop turning it into a kind of hobby. But after some time I got really interested on how the actual websites are made and decided to learn more about the front-end development. I successfully took a Front-End Development Course and I will continue studying and grow because I now know that this is the field I want to be professional in. This makes me feel completely satisfied and helpful which is very important If it is going to be my profession/job. Thank you for your attention and I hope you will like my works.
